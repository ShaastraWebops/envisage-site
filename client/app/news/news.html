About us

6 Editions . 7 Shows . 5000 People

The Annual Technical Festival of IIT Madras, Shaastra introduced a technologically aided cultural show in the year 2013, capitalizing on the entertainment factor. That techno – cultural show was named Envisage and today it is India’s first and only student organised techno-entertainment show. The projects which were exhibited in former editions of Envisage attracted a bunch of not-so-technically oriented guests too. The projects were designed in conformity with the tagline of Envisage – “Technology meets Entertainment”. Organisers of Envisage decided to widen its boundaries beyond Shaastra and founded a club under CFI. The club is known as Techno-Entertainment Club and has been functional for the past one year.

Five Years of Existence

Envisage is the jewel in the crown that is Shaastra. It remains the only student-orchestrated technical show in its domain and is among the spectacles that Shaastra produces every year. Envisage entail a series of techno-entertainment performances involving a tantalizing synchronization of the lights and sound. Through projects such as Free Illusion, Virtual Instruments, Water-Fall and Wiremap, Envisage has mesmerized audience over the five years of its existence. From our sponsors to media persons, and students to event panelists, plenty have been wowed by the sparkling event, and it is therefore not a surprise that the show has been run twice to accommodate the crowds that amass at the show arena for the performances. Envisage has played host to audiences sizes of 5000 for 7 shows in six successful editions. These are unprecedented numbers for a show that boasts to be one of its kind in the country.

About Shaastra

Shaastra is the annual technical festival of IIT Madras. True to its name, the sanskrit equivalent of science, Shaastra is a celebration of unconstrained thinking and love for the spirit of engineering. A cauldron of diverse disciplines in science, it provides the opportunity to catch a glimpse at the latest technology that is trending worldwide. Having been established in 2000, Shaastra is back with its 18th edition this year. A completely student run techno-managerial festival, Shaastra stretches over four nights and days in the first week of January. The fest holds the distinction of being the first student managed event in the world to implement a Quality Management System and earn the ISO 9001:2000 certification. This has further been upgraded twice to ISO 9001:2008 and ISO 9001:2015 certifications. This stands as a testament to the impeccable standards of the festival over the years.

About CFI

Centre for Innovation (CFI) is a ‘Student Lab’ at IIT Madras. Set up with funds donated by the batch of 1981, it is set in the building that houses the Central Workshop, right in the middle of the academic zone in the beautiful IIT Madras Campus. CFI was started in 2008 with an objective to be a forum for creative output of the budding engineers of IIT Madras. It provides students the necessary platform for realizing their ideas. A great workspace, a comprehensive inventory, ample guidance from faculty & senior-students and the zeal and passion of the CFI team make CFI a gifted asset for IIT Madras students. And over time, CFI has successfully evolved into a hub which encourages thinking and provokes students to generate novel ideas making it truly the “centre” for innovation.
